Noodles



Description

Noodles are a staple food in many cultures and made into a variety of shapes.Noodles are usually cooked in boiling water, sometimes with cooking oil or salt added. They are often pan-fried or deep-fried. Noodles are often served with an accompanying sauce or in a soup.

Ingredients :

  • Plain noodles
  • Capsicum
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Egg
  • Soya sauce
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps :

  1. Boil 6 cups water in a large pot or a deep pan and bring it to boil over medium flame. When it comes to rolling boil, add dried noodles, 1/2 tablespoon oil and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Boil until noodles are soft, it will take around 4-5 minutes. Stir occasionally in between.
  2. Now heat 2 to 3 tablespoons of toasted sesame oil in a wok or pan. Keep the heat to low or medium-low. Add 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic and 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger.
  3. After that, keep on stirring continuously and stir fry the onions until they become translucent.
  4. Then add the veggies: ½ cup shredded carrots, ⅓ cup thinly sliced capsicum and 1 cup shredded cabbage.
  5. Some crunch should be there in the vegetables, so don’t cook the vegetables until they become completely soft.
  6. Add required amount of salt, soya sauce and pepper.
  7. Keep on tossing and stirring till the veggies are mixed well for minute or two. Switch off the heat. Check the taste and add more salt, black pepper or soy sauce if required to the veggie noodles.
