Chicken Nuggets



Description

A chicken nugget is a food product consisting of a small piece of deboned chicken meat that is breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked. Invented in the 1950s, chicken nuggets have become a very popular fast food restaurant item, as well as widely sold frozen for home use

Ingredients :

  • Chicken(Potato for veg)
  • Oil
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  • Mixed herbs
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt

Steps :

  1. wash ands clean the boneless chicken, pat dry and marinate the chicken. To marinate the chicken take a bowl and whisk around 1/2 cup hung curd along with salt, pepper, ginger paste, garlic paste and red chilli powder.
  2. Arrange the ingredients once the pieces are marinated wellMake a smooth paste, marinate the chicken and keep it in a refrigerator for an hour.
  3. Once the meat is nicely marinated. Begin with the cooking process, then take three bowl and fill them separately with whisked egg, flour mixture made with salt, garlic powder, pepper, mixed herbs and paprika; Lastly, a bowl full of breadcrumbs.
  4. Next dip the boneless chicken in egg batter followed by seasoned flour; lastly in bread crumbs.
  5. Repeat with all the pieces. In the meantime, heat a deep bottomed pan and add oil for shallow frying. Once the oil is hot enough gently slide the nuggets.
  6. Shallow fry the nuggets, till they turn golden in colour and serve hot with a dip or sauce of your choice and enjoy
